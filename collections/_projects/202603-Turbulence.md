The 2026 UKTC annual meeting will be held in Edinburgh, Scotland, on the 26th and 27th March 2026, the meeting will start at 10am on the first day and will end at 4pm on the second day. There will be a dinner organised on the first day, in the lovely Playfair Library Hall. 

The meeting will consist of one keynote lecture, oral presentations, panel sessions and an interactive poster session. 

Location of the meeting: Pollock Estate in the South Hall.

UKTC ARCHER2 users are expected to present their work at the meeting with an oral presentation or a poster (only a title and a short 250-word abstract are required for the registration).

REGISTRATION can be found [​here](https://forms.office.com/e/BdtbsNjEJ4)

The UKTC will cover the costs of attending the meeting for its members and their students (up to two hotel nigh per person, max £111 per room per night, and economy train/flight tickets), for more details see the event website.
